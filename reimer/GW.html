Delivering the goodness of nature to a global community

What We Do

At Global Widget, we design, develop and distribute natural health and wellness products. Our process begins with market research and product formulation to develop premium products that make people feel their best. We focus on the healing properties found in nature, which is why we use natural, organic ingredients grown locally in the United States. We then manufacture our products in our in-house production facility – paying close attention to packaging, design and usability. From there, our products are shipped to distributors and stores around the world, and we also ship directly to consumers. With professionals in design, marketing and sales, we build upon our network to help our products reach as many stores, doctor's offices and doorsteps as possible.

Who We Are

We're creative and passionate professionals on a mission – to deliver top-notch natural health products to a global community. Our diverse team includes experts in all areas of product manufacturing and distribution, including formulation, shipping, inventory, marketing, web development and more. We're one of the biggest players in our segment of the health market, and we are constantly learning, evolving and improving our business efforts and personal talents. We set strict quality and purity standards that are unsurpassed by our competitors – all to provide the very best products for maximum health benefits. We're a devoted team with passion for our products, and respect for one another.

Our Mission

At Global Widget, it's our mission to promote healthier lifestyles by delivering natural products to a global community. We strive to lead our industry in design, development and distribution through in-house expertise and innovation.

Our Departments

Manufacturing

As Global Widget's largest team, manufacturing is the heart of our business. All products are manufactured in-house with a commitment to quality. Our master formulators craft each product for maximum health and wellness benefits.

Sales

Our sales professionals connect our premium wellness products with distributors, stores and local businesses by building lasting relationships. They continue to build upon a robust distribution network across the country through genuine B2B marketing and selling.

HR & Operations

With our mission statement and core values as our guiding principles, our HR & Operations team is committed to employee engagement and retention. We invest in our employees' health, talent, financial security, and career development.

eCommerce

One of our fastest-growing departments, our Global eCommerce department includes website development, graphic design, social media, content marketing, communications, affiliate marketing, customer service and more.

Shipping & Inventory

We ship products all over the world, and our shipping team understands that speed and efficiency are top priorities. Our inventory experts keep our warehouses stocked and organized for timely fulfillment.

Compliance & Legal

When operating in the health and wellness industry, it's important to stay abreast of current policies and best practices. Our legal and compliance team provides valuable support in helping us establish policies, contracts, processes and more.
